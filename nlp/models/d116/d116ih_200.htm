[2] Once in orbit above Earth's distorting atmosphere, Hubble would be able to see 10 times better than the largest Earth telescopes

[8] The shuttle followed Hubble for two days to assist if necessary
[9] A seven- month checkout period would follow
[10] Spacelab Astro was to be put up two weeks later to give Hubble additional target information
[11] Hubble, delayed since 1983, cost $1.5 billion
